Product: samsung S21 ultra

Description:


Samsung Galaxy S21 is a series of Android-based smartphones designed, developed, marketed, and made by Samsung Electronics as part of its Galaxy S series. They are after the Galaxy S20 series. The first three were announced at Samsung's Galaxy Unpacked event on 14 January 2021 while the FE was annouced on 3 January 2022. The first three phones were released on 29 January 2021 while the FE was released on 11 January 2022.
Samsung Galaxy S21's design is similar to the one before, Samsung Galaxy S20. The rear cameras have been made part of the phone body; the S21 Ultra has carbon fiber for special colors.
